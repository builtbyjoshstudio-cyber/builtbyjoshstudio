Refund Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Tynkr Tools & Co sells digital products including Notion templates, Excel workbooks, and Google Sheets workbooks. Because these products are delivered instantly and cannot be returned once accessed, all sales are final.

All Sales Are Final

Due to the digital nature of our products, we do not issue refunds once a product has been downloaded, accessed, or duplicated. This applies to all products sold through our Etsy shop, Lemon Squeezy store, and builtbyjoshstudio.com.

This policy is consistent with standard digital product practices and is disclosed at the point of purchase.

Technical Issues

If you experience a technical issue with your product — such as a broken download link, a file that won't open, or a Notion template that won't duplicate — contact us at tynkrtoolsco@gmail.com and we will resolve it promptly. Technical issues are always addressed.

Exceptions

Refunds may be issued where required by applicable consumer protection law in your jurisdiction. If you believe you are entitled to a refund under the laws of your country or region, contact us at tynkrtoolsco@gmail.com and we will review your request.

Purchasing Through Etsy

Contact
