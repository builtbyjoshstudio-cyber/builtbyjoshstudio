Frequently Asked

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Tynkr Tools & Co and Built By Josh Studio?

Built By Josh Studio is the parent studio. Tynkr Tools & Co is the digital productivity brand — Notion OS templates and Excel/Google Sheets workbooks. Built By Josh Studio also publishes zodiac digital art prints. Same studio, two distinct product lines.

Where do I buy the templates and spreadsheets?

All Tynkr Tools & Co templates and zodiac art prints are sold through Etsy at builtbyjoshstudio.etsy.com. You'll get an instant digital download after purchase — no accounts, no waiting.

Do the Notion templates work with the free version of Notion?

Yes. Every Notion OS template works on both the free and paid versions of Notion. You don't need a subscription to use them.

Can I use the budget spreadsheets in both Excel and Google Sheets?

Most spreadsheet products are available as an Excel version, a Google Sheets version, or a bundle that includes both. Check the individual product page for formats.

Do I get updates after I buy?

Yes. Every product includes free lifetime updates. When a new version ships, you'll get access to it at no additional cost.

Who is Josh?

Josh is a solo creator based in Kansas. He builds every Notion template, spreadsheet, and zodiac print in the studio, and is the author of Overlayed Echoes — his debut fantasy detective noir novel — and the in-progress Ebonspire Chronicles series.
