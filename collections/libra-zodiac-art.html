What Makes Libra, Libra

Libra is the seventh sign of the zodiac, spanning September 23 through October 22. It is an air sign ruled by Venus — the planet of beauty, love, and aesthetic value. Libra is represented by the scales, the only inanimate symbol in the zodiac — a sign defined not by instinct or force but by the pursuit of balance.

People born under Libra are often described as diplomatic, aesthetically driven, and deeply attuned to fairness. They seek harmony in their relationships, their environments, and even the visual composition of their living spaces. Libra energy is measured, intentional, and always considering both sides — but beneath the composure is a sign that cares fiercely about justice.

That tension between beauty and consequence runs through this entire collection. Whether expressed through symmetrical sacred geometry, the cold authority of The Celestial Arbiter, or the haunting judgment of The Weighing of Souls — every Libra piece is designed to look perfectly composed while carrying real weight underneath.
