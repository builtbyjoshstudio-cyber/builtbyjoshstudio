What Makes Taurus, Taurus

Taurus is the second sign of the zodiac, spanning April 20 through May 20. It is an earth sign ruled by Venus — the planet of beauty, pleasure, and material value. Taurus is represented by the bull, a symbol of endurance, determination, and grounded strength.

People born under Taurus are often described as reliable, patient, and deeply sensory. They value quality over quantity, comfort over chaos, and tend to build things meant to last. Taurus energy is slow and deliberate — it doesn't rush, but it doesn't quit either.

That steadiness runs through every piece in this collection. Whether expressed through rich gold sacred geometry, the weight of a dark fantasy titan, or the soft permanence of a watercolor wash — every Taurus print is designed to feel anchored and intentional, the way the sign itself moves through the world.
