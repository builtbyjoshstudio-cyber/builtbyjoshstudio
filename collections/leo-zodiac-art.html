What Makes Leo, Leo

Leo is the fifth sign of the zodiac, spanning July 23 through August 22. It is a fire sign ruled by the Sun — the only sign in the zodiac governed by a star rather than a planet. Leo is represented by the lion, a symbol of sovereignty, courage, and the kind of presence that doesn't need to announce itself.

People born under Leo are often described as confident, generous, and magnetically warm. They lead naturally — not by force but by gravity. People orient around them. Leo energy is solar: it radiates outward, it lights up a room, and it expects the room to notice.

That commanding, luminous quality defines this entire collection. Whether rendered in sacred geometry and gold leaf, the cinematic weight of The Solar Emperor, or the molten fury of the Hell-Lion — every Leo piece is designed to feel like the centerpiece of whatever wall it hangs on. Nothing in this collection was made to blend in.
