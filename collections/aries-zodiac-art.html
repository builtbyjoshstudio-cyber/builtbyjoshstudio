What Makes Aries, Aries

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, spanning March 21 through April 19. It is a fire sign ruled by Mars — the planet of drive, aggression, and action. Aries is represented by the ram, a symbol of headfirst momentum and refusal to back down.

People born under Aries are often described as bold, competitive, and direct. They tend to be initiators — the ones who start the project, make the first move, and set the pace. Aries energy is forward motion. It doesn't wait for permission and it doesn't look back.

That energy defines this entire collection. Whether rendered in sacred geometry and gold leaf or in neon cyberpunk circuitry, every Aries piece is designed to feel like it's moving — even standing still.
