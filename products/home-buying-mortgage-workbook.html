What Is the Home Buying & Mortgage Workbook?

The Home Buying & Mortgage Workbook is a home buying spreadsheet with 15 pre-built tabs that cover every financial decision in the home buying process — from affordability analysis to closing day and beyond. Available for both Excel and Google Sheets. You enter your core financial data once in the Control Panel, and most tabs calculate automatically. A few tabs have additional inputs for scenario-specific analysis.

The workbook includes an Affordability Dashboard with debt-to-income analysis, a Rent vs Buy break-even calculator over 5, 10, and 20-year horizons, a Down Payment savings tracker, a side-by-side Mortgage Comparison for three loan scenarios, a full month-by-month Amortization Schedule with auto PMI drop, an Extra Payments impact calculator, a 23-line-item Closing Costs estimator with estimate vs actual columns, a weighted Home Search Scorer for comparing up to five properties, a First-Year Homeowner Budget for expenses most buyers forget, a Maintenance Reserve tracker for big-ticket repair planning, and a Net Worth Projector comparing buying vs renting and investing. It also includes a Beginner Guide for first-time buyers and a Pro Strategy Guide covering DTI optimization, ARM vs fixed analysis, and points strategy.

Who It's For

The Home Buying & Mortgage Workbook is built for:

  • First-time home buyers who want to understand what they can actually afford before talking to a lender — not after.
  • Anyone comparing mortgage options who needs to see three loan scenarios side by side with total cost, monthly payment, and interest differences clearly laid out.
  • Renters deciding whether to buy who want a real break-even analysis — not a rule of thumb — over 5, 10, and 20-year horizons.
  • Buyers saving for a down payment who want to track progress toward their goal with a timeline and monthly contribution targets.
  • Homeowners in their first year who need a realistic budget that includes the expenses most buyers forget — maintenance, utilities, setup costs, and HOA fees.

What's Inside

Fifteen tabs covering every stage of the home buying process. Here's what you get:

Control Panel + Guides

One Setup, Everything Calculates

Enter your income, debts, savings, target home price, interest rate, and loan term on the Control Panel. Most tabs calculate from these inputs automatically. The Beginner Guide walks first-time buyers through the workbook step by step. The Pro Guide covers advanced strategies like DTI optimization, points analysis, and ARM vs fixed decision frameworks.

Affordability Dashboard

Know Before You Shop

Visual analysis of your debt-to-income ratios — housing-to-income, total DTI, and FHA maximum. Each metric shows your value, the guideline, the max allowed, and a pass/fail status. See your monthly housing cost and whether your budget can handle it before you start looking at listings.

Rent vs Buy & Down Payment

The Real Comparison

The Rent vs Buy calculator compares total cost of buying versus renting and investing the difference over 5, 10, and 20 years — factoring in appreciation, rent increases, investment returns, and selling costs. The Down Payment tracker shows your savings goal, current balance, monthly contribution, and projected timeline to reach your target.

Mortgage Comparison & Amortization

Three Loans, Full Visibility

Compare three mortgage scenarios side by side — different rates, terms, down payments, or loan types. See monthly payment, total interest, total cost, and break-even point for each. The Amortization tab shows a full month-by-month schedule with principal, interest, balance, and automatic PMI drop when you hit 80% loan-to-value.

Extra Payments & Closing Costs

See the Impact of Every Dollar

The Extra Payments calculator shows how additional monthly or annual lump-sum payments reduce total interest and shorten your loan term. The Closing Costs estimator itemizes 23 line items across lender fees, title charges, prepaid items, and government fees — with estimate and actual columns so you can compare against your Loan Estimate document.

Home Scorer, First-Year Budget & Maintenance

Compare, Budget, and Plan Ahead

The Home Search Scorer lets you rate and compare up to five properties on weighted criteria you define — location, price, size, condition, and more. The First-Year Budget covers expenses most buyers forget: utilities, setup costs, HOA, lawn care, and maintenance. The Maintenance Reserve tracker projects replacement timelines and costs for every major home system — roof, HVAC, water heater, appliances — based on age and expected lifespan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work in Google Sheets or just Excel?

Both. The workbook is available as an Excel file and a Google Sheets version. You can buy either individually for $24.99 or get both in the bundle for $34.99. All formulas and formatting work in both platforms.

Is this for first-time buyers or experienced buyers?

Both. The workbook includes a Beginner Guide with step-by-step instructions and a Pro Strategy Guide covering advanced topics like DTI optimization, ARM vs fixed analysis, and points strategy. Use the tabs that match where you are in the process.

Can I compare different mortgage options?

Yes. The Mortgage Comparison tab lets you enter three loan scenarios with different rates, terms, down payments, or loan types and see monthly payment, total interest, and total cost side by side. Loan 1 pulls from the Control Panel automatically. Loans 2 and 3 have their own input fields.

No. This workbook is a financial analysis and planning tool. It helps you understand your numbers, compare scenarios, and make informed decisions. It does not constitute financial, tax, or legal advice. Always work with qualified professionals for your specific situation.

Will I get updates if the workbook improves?

Yes. Lifetime updates are included. When a new version is released, you'll receive access to the updated file.

Can I customize the tabs and formulas?

Yes. Input cells are open for editing. Formula cells are protected by default to prevent accidental changes, but you can unlock them if needed. The workbook is yours to customize once you download it.
