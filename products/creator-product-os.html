What Is Creator Product OS?

Creator Product OS is a Notion product catalog template for creators who sell digital products and need one workspace to manage their entire inventory — products, files, platform listings, bundles, version history, and support issues. It replaces the mental model of "I think that file is somewhere in Google Drive" with a connected system where every asset, listing, and update ties back to a specific product record.

The template includes six modules built around a central Products database. Product Files & Assets tracks every file, link, mockup, and deliverable connected to a product. Listings & Platforms tracks where each product is sold and the status of each storefront entry. Bundles manages grouped offers. Version Log documents every update and patch. Support & Issues flags bugs, broken links, and maintenance items. A Catalog Snapshot dashboard on the homepage surfaces your active catalog, products needing attention, drafts in progress, and recent version updates at a glance. Setup takes roughly ten minutes.

What's Inside

Six connected modules built around your Products hub. Here's what you get:

Products

Your Master Catalog

The central hub of the system. Every digital product you sell or are building gets a record here — with status, type, version, price, and links to all related files, listings, bundles, and issues. Everything else in the system connects back to a product.

Product Files & Assets

Every File Accounted For

Track every file, link, thumbnail, mockup, guide, ZIP, and delivery asset connected to a product. Each entry has a status — Ready, Missing, Needs Update, or Deprecated — so you can see at a glance what's current and what needs work.

Listings & Platforms

Where You Sell, All in One View

Track every storefront listing for every product. Whether you sell on Etsy, Shopify, or your own site, each listing has its own record with status, URL, and linked product. A Maintenance Queue view flags listings that need updates.

Bundles

Grouped Offers Under Control

Manage bundle products and track which individual items are included in each. When a component product gets updated, you can see which bundles are affected and need attention.

Version Log

Every Update Documented

Record every update, patch, and version change for any product. Each entry links to the product it applies to and includes what changed and when. Builds a complete history so you never wonder what version is live or what was fixed last month.

Support & Issues

Catch Problems Before Customers Do

Flag bugs, broken links, missing files, and feature requests. Each issue links to a product and has a status — Open, In Progress, or Resolved. A High Priority Queue on the homepage surfaces what needs immediate attention.

Why This Instead of a Folder or Spreadsheet

A folder full of product files tells you what exists. It doesn't tell you which files are current, which listings need updating, or which products have unresolved issues. Creator Product OS connects every file, listing, and version to the product it belongs to — so one glance at a product record shows you everything attached to it.

Spreadsheets can list your products, but they can't flag that a listing on Etsy is out of date while the same product on your website is current. They can't show you that a bundle contains three products and one of them just got a version update. This system can, because every record is relational.

This is a product operations system — purpose-built for creators who ship digital products across multiple platforms and need to keep every piece of the catalog clean, connected, and current.

Frequently Asked

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a paid Notion account to use Creator Product OS?

No. Creator Product OS works with the free version of Notion. You can duplicate it to your workspace and start using it immediately without upgrading.

Is this template customizable?

Fully. Every database, property, view, status, and category can be renamed, rearranged, or removed. The template gives you a working product operations structure — you own it once you duplicate it and can adjust it to match your catalog.

Is this the same as a project management tool?

No. Creator Product OS is specifically for product operations — tracking what you sell, what state it's in, and what needs maintenance. It does not handle content planning, launch execution, or client management. Those are covered by other templates in the Creator OS line.

Can I track products on multiple platforms?

Yes. The Listings & Platforms module lets you create a separate listing record for each platform a product is sold on. One product can have an Etsy listing, a website listing, and a Shopify listing — all tracked individually with their own status.

Will I get updates if the template improves?

Yes. Lifetime updates are included. When a new version is released, you'll receive access to the updated template. Your existing data stays in your workspace — updates apply to new duplications.

I only have a few products right now. Is this worth it?

Yes. The best time to set up a product operations system is when your catalog is small enough to get organized in one sitting. As your product count grows, you'll already have a clean foundation — every file accounted for, every listing tracked, every update logged.
