What Is Creator Launch OS?

Creator Launch OS is a Notion launch planner for creators who need one workspace to manage every moving piece of a product launch — timelines, tasks, assets, promo content, and the offers behind them. It replaces scattered checklists and last-minute scrambles with a connected system where every task and asset ties back to a specific launch record.

The template includes five core modules: a Launches database that serves as the master record for every campaign, a Launch Tasks tracker for every action item needed to execute, an Assets module for graphics, copy, links, and files, a Launch Content planner for promo posts and emails, and an Offers & Products catalog that defines what you're selling. A Launch Command Center on the homepage surfaces active launches, tasks due this week, missing assets, and queued content at a glance. Every launch moves through six defined phases — Planning, Asset Creation, Pre-Launch, Launch Week, Post-Launch, and Wrapped — so you always know exactly where a campaign stands.

Who It's For

Creator Launch OS is built for:

  • Digital product sellers launching Notion templates, spreadsheets, printables, or ebooks who need a repeatable system instead of reinventing the process every time.
  • Course creators and coaches running enrollment windows or cohort launches who need to track tasks, assets, and promo content against a hard deadline.
  • Creators running seasonal promos or limited-time offers who need to see what's ready, what's missing, and what's overdue before launch day.
  • Solo entrepreneurs juggling multiple product lines who want one workspace to manage launches across different offers without mixing them up.
  • Anyone who has launched something and wished they had started organizing earlier — this is the system that makes the next one smoother.

What's Inside

Five connected modules plus a command center. Here's what you get:

Launches

The Master Record for Every Campaign

Every launch, promo, or campaign you run gets one record here. Set the name, type, status, phase, launch date, and link it to your offer. This becomes the anchor — tasks, assets, and content all connect back to it. One launch record per campaign keeps everything clean.

Launch Tasks

Every Action Item in One Place

The execution layer. Every to-do, deliverable, or action item needed to run a launch lives here, linked to its parent launch. Set due dates, priorities, and statuses. A homepage view surfaces tasks due this week so deadlines stay visible without digging through individual records.

Assets

Everything You Need Ready Before Go-Live

Track every resource required for a launch — graphics, copy blocks, checkout links, bonuses, and files. Each asset has a status so you can see at a glance what's done, what's in progress, and what's still missing. The homepage flags incomplete assets automatically.

Launch Content

Your Promo Plan Tied to the Launch

Track the promotional content pieces tied to each launch — Instagram posts, emails, reels, pins, and threads. Set publish dates and link each piece to its launch. If you also use Creator Content OS, keep your full content library there and use this module only for launch-specific promo tracking.

Offers & Products

What You're Actually Launching

A simple catalog that defines what you're selling or promoting. Name, type, price, and platform — enough to anchor every launch to a clear offer. This is not a full product management system. It's the reference point that gives each launch its purpose.

Launch Command Center

Open the System and Know Immediately

The homepage dashboard pulls live views from every module: active launches, tasks due this week, missing or incomplete assets, and queued launch content. Open the system, scan the dashboard, and know exactly what needs attention today.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a paid Notion account to use Creator Launch OS?

No. Creator Launch OS works with the free version of Notion. You can duplicate it to your workspace and start using it immediately without upgrading.

Is this template customizable?

Fully. Every database, property, view, phase, and status can be renamed, rearranged, or removed. The template provides a working launch structure — you own it once you duplicate it and can adjust it to match how you run campaigns.

Can I use this for multiple launches at the same time?

Yes. Each launch gets its own master record, and all tasks, assets, and content link to their specific launch. The Launch Command Center filters by active launches so you can track multiple campaigns without mixing them up.

How does this work with Creator Content OS?

They complement each other. Creator Content OS manages your full content library and ongoing publishing rhythm. Creator Launch OS tracks only the promo content tied to a specific launch campaign. Keep your everyday content workflow in Content OS and use Launch OS when you have a campaign to execute.

Will I get updates if the template improves?

Yes. Lifetime updates are included. When a new version is released, you'll receive access to the updated template. Your existing data stays in your workspace — updates apply to new duplications.

I've never launched a product before. Is this beginner-friendly?

Yes. The six-phase workflow gives you a clear path from Planning to Wrapped. The Start Here page walks you through setting up your first launch in six steps. Sample data is included so you can see exactly how a fully connected launch looks before building your own.
